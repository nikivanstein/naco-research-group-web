Venue

FOGA 2025 will be held at the new Gorleaus building, Einsteinweg 55, 2333 CC Leiden, The Netherlands. The university is located near the centre of Leiden, providing easy access to various transport options.

First Arrival

When arriving by train: From Leiden Central Station, you can take a bus or a short walk to the new Gorleaus building. Detailed instructions on reaching the campus will be provided closer to the conference date.

Rooms

All of FOGA will take place on the first floor. Follow the FOGA signs in the Gorleaus building.

On-site Services

  • Coffee break: Foyer on the ground floor of Gorlaeus (turn right before the building reception)
  • Lunch: Brasserie Science on the ground floor of Gorlaeus (turn right before the building reception). Lunch is at the expense of the participants. Debit or credit card payments only.
  • Reception: Drinks and snacks will be offered at the De Fusie bar on the ground floor of Gorlaeus (turn left after the building reception)
  • Dinner travel: Attendees will need to take public transport to the dinner venue (about 20 mins bus ride, debit or credit card payments only). Details to be announced soon
  • Dinner: Dinner is included in the registration fee. Details to be announced soon

Hotel Recommendations

  • Hilton Garden Inn (5 minutes walking)
  • Golden Tulip Leiden Centre (15 minutes by public transport)
  • IBIS Leiden Centre (10 minutes by public transport)
  • Van Der Valk Hotel Leiden (20 minutes walking)

Presentation Format

Digital Posters

  • Format: Landscape, 100 x 70 cm
  • Equipment: Please bring your own laptop (with HDMI or USB-C ports, or the appropriate adapters) to connect to the display screens.
  • Setup: Your poster will be projected during your assigned session.

Printed Posters

  • If you prefer a printed poster, please inform us in advance so we can arrange space.
  • Format recommendation: A0 size (poster stands will support both portrait and landscape).
  • Info: You may bring posters printed at home or on cloth/canvas for easier transport. Local printing options in Leiden are also available (see poster printing info).

Financial Travel Support

Young Researcher and Innovator Conference Grant

Researchers attending FOGA 2025 may apply for financial travel support through the Young Researcher and Innovator Conference Grant. Below are the details for eligibility and application:
  • Applicants must be under the age of 40
  • Applicants must have an accepted paper at FOGA 2025
  • Primary affiliation must be with a legal entity located in a COST Full/Cooperating Member or Near Neighbour Country (NNC). Refer to the Country and Organisations Table for details.
For additional details and application instructions, visit the official Young Researcher and Innovator Conference Grant Call.
