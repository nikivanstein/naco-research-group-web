Presentation Format

Digital Posters

  • Format: Landscape, 100 x 70 cm
  • Equipment: Please bring your own laptop (with HDMI or USB-C ports, or the appropriate adapters) to connect to the display screens.
  • Setup: Your poster will be projected during your assigned session.

Printed Posters

  • If you prefer a printed poster, please inform us in advance so we can arrange space.
  • Format recommendation: A0 size (poster stands will support both portrait and landscape).
  • Info: You may bring posters printed at home or on cloth/canvas for easier transport. Local printing options in Leiden are also available (see poster printing info).
